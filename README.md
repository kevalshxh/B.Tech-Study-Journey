# Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

## Overview

The **Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)** is a four-year undergraduate engineering degree designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science, mathematics, engineering principles, and modern technology. The program combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, enabling students to develop analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills required to address real-world engineering challenges.

A B.Tech curriculum emphasizes innovation, research, project-based learning, and industry-oriented training. Students gain hands-on experience through laboratory sessions, internships, workshops, and collaborative projects, preparing them for careers in engineering, technology, research, entrepreneurship, and higher education.

---

## Program Objectives

The B.Tech program aims to:

- Build a strong foundation in engineering fundamentals.
- Develop analytical and critical thinking skills.
- Enhance problem-solving and decision-making abilities.
- Encourage innovation, creativity, and research.
- Provide practical exposure through laboratories and industry projects.
- Prepare students for professional careers and higher studies.
- Foster ethical responsibility and lifelong learning.

## Duration

- **Degree:** Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
- **Duration:** 4 Years
